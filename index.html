Universiti Teknologi Mara

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is Malaysia's premier institution of higher learning, offering a wide range of programs designed to produce skilled, knowledgeable, and competent graduates. UiTM is dedicated to excellence in education and fostering innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among its students.
